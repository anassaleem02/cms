Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our solar products and services.

We offer a comprehensive 5-year warranty on all our inverters and lithium batteries, and a 15-year warranty on solar panels. Our warranty covers manufacturing defects and performance issues. We provide hassle-free replacement or repair services during the warranty period.
Yes, we provide professional installation services across Karachi and surrounding areas. Our certified technicians ensure safe and efficient installation with minimal disruption. Installation charges vary based on system size and complexity. Contact us for a free site assessment and quote.
The right inverter size depends on your total power consumption and the appliances you want to run. As a general guide: 1.6KW is suitable for small homes with basic appliances, 4KW for medium homes with ACs, and 6.2KW for larger homes or small businesses. Contact us for a free consultation - we'll analyze your electricity bills and recommend the perfect solution.
Yes! All our inverters come with smart monitoring capabilities. You can download our mobile app (available for iOS and Android) to track real-time power generation, consumption, battery status, and system health from anywhere. You'll also receive alerts for any issues or maintenance requirements.
We accept cash payments, bank transfers, and easy monthly installment plans through various banks. For larger systems, we can arrange financing options. Contact us to discuss the payment plan that works best for you. A deposit is required to confirm your order, with the balance due upon installation.
For a standard residential installation, the process typically takes 1-2 days depending on the system size and site conditions. This includes mounting solar panels, installing the inverter and battery system, and configuring the monitoring system. Larger commercial installations may take longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the site assessment.
